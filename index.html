Who We Are

JGroup is a pioneering global media & technology group, redefining the boundaries of brand connection through cutting-edge innovation, data intelligence, & trusted global alliances

Headquartered in Dubai, JGroup operates across 35+ countries through 21 offices, driving growth in Technology, Data, AI, Global Media Representation, Out-of-Home, Content & Production

Committed to advancing the digital economy across MENA, JGroup serves as the exclusive & official media representative for leading international platforms, including Google Ads, TikTok, Amazon Ads, Snapchat, Channel Factory, Blis, Criteo, & iHeart. Through these strategic alliances, we empower brands with data-driven marketing solutions that connect them to audiences in smarter, more meaningful ways, accelerating digital transformation across the region
