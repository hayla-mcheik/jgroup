Who We Are

JGROUP is a global media and technology powerhouse, partnering with the world's leading brands and platforms to deliver innovation with impact. From its regional headquarters in Dubai, JGroup operates across 35+ countries through 21 offices, driving growth across e-commerce, content and production, out-of-home media, technology, data, AI, global media representation and real estate.

Always aiming to bring the best global solutions to grow the digital economy in the region, JGroup partners with leading international platforms as the exclusive and official media representative, including iHeart, Google Ads, TikTok, Amazon Ads, Snapchat, Channel Factory, Blis, and Criteo.

Through these partnerships, JGroup empowers brands with data-driven solutions that connect them to audiences in smarter, more meaningful ways and help accelerate digital growth across MENA
